Founder

Built from inside finance.

Most automation agencies don't understand your compliance requirements, your data flows, or your operational constraints. We do — because we come from the industry.

Sajan Goswami founded SierraFlow after seeing firsthand how financial services firms operate — and where they lose the most time.

As a CMBS Analyst at KBRA (Kroll Bond Rating Agency), Sajan analyzed commercial mortgage-backed securities, built data dashboards from SEC filings, and understood the compliance and operational challenges these firms face daily.

That domain expertise, combined with hands-on experience in agentic AI engineering and financial software development, is what makes SierraFlow different. We understand the workflows before we automate them.
